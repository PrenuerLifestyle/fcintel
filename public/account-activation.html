FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Why was my account suspended without explanation?
Platforms often use automated systems that flag accounts incorrectly. A well-documented appeal addressing known trigger reasons significantly improves reinstatement rates.
Can you recover funds held in a restricted account?
In many cases yes — once the account restriction is lifted, all held funds are released. We coordinate the full process.
What if my appeal was already rejected?
A rejected appeal is not the end. We submit new evidence-backed appeals with different framing that addresses the underlying reason for rejection.
How long does account activation take?
Simple cases resolve in 3–7 days. Complex KYC or compliance cases can take 2–6 weeks. We provide regular progress updates.
