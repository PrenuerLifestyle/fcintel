FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

My email was changed — can you still recover my account?
Yes. Platforms have processes for recovering accounts where the email has been changed by an unauthorized party. We specialize in these cases.
Can you recover an account that was deleted?
Deleted accounts can sometimes be recovered within the platform's deletion grace period (usually 30 days). Act fast.
What about hacked accounts used for spam?
We can help recover the account and deal with any associated spam flags or suspensions that occurred while the hacker was in control.
Do I need to provide my login details to you?
Never. We work exclusively through official platform channels and never require your passwords or personal login credentials.
